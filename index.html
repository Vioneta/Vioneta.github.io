    • Our Products

    At Vioneta Agro, quality stands as the cornerstone of our product philosophy. We engineer each component of our agricultural solutions with an unwavering commitment to excellence, ensuring robustness, reliability, and resilience in every facet of our technology. Our products are meticulously crafted to thrive in the demanding conditions of agricultural settings. Built to endure harsh climates, rugged terrains, and variable environmental factors, they boast exceptional durability and resilience, guaranteeing uninterrupted performance even in the most challenging scenarios.

    Vioneta Agro OS

    A specialized operating system managing the Vioneta Agro Edge Platform's low-level services, local storage, updates, Docker container management, network configurations, and local hardware components like Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and LoRa connectivity.

    Vioneta Agro Automation Controller

    An executor of predefined automation tasks configured by the Vioneta Agro Edge Computer. It executes specific agricultural automation sequences and tasks orchestrated by the Edge Computer, contributing to enhanced operational efficiency.

    Vioneta Agro Edge Computer

    Equipped with Vioneta Agro OS, the Edge Computer serves as the central hub, managing multiple Automation Controllers. It orchestrates automation processes, coordinates tasks, and communicates with interconnected devices and sensors across the agricultural infrastructure.

    Vioneta Agro Lora Gateway

    Acts as a central hub, enabling LoRa-based communication between the Edge Computer and interconnected hardware components, sensors, and Automation Controllers.

    Vioneta Agro Cloud

    A cloud-based service allowing secure remote access to the Edge Computer from anywhere, providing monitoring, data analysis, configuration, and control functionalities.

    Vioneta Agro Marketplace

    The Vioneta Agro Marketplace stands as a comprehensive repository comprising dockerized applications, plugins, themes, UI components, and AI models. It serves as an invaluable resource for solution builders and developers seeking to enhance and extend the capabilities of the Vioneta Agro ecosystem.

    Vioneta Agro Mobile App

    An application enabling users to access and manage the Edge Computer locally or remotely. It offers a user-friendly interface for monitoring, control, and data analysis.
