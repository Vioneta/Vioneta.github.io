Our Solution

Vioneta Agro Platform delivers a comprehensive, scalable, and intelligent infrastructure for modern agriculture.

  • Proprietary Edge Computing: Enables local, offline data processing, providing farmers with real-time insights regardless of connectivity.
  • Centralized Data Hub: Collects, processes, and translates complex machine and sensor data into clear, actionable information for critical decision-making.
  • Seamless Integration: Integrates sensors, cameras, agriculture machinery, AI-enabled decision support systems with chatbots, machine learning algorithms, data analytics, and automation to optimize irrigation, fertilization, and crop monitoring.
  • Vioneta Academy: Complemented by our academy, we provide structured training and support to ensure successful adoption and maximize the impact of our technology on farm productivity and sustainability.

Our Products

At Vioneta Agro, quality stands as the cornerstone of our product philosophy. We engineer each component of our agricultural solutions with an unwavering commitment to excellence, ensuring robustness, reliability, and resilience in every facet of our technology. Our products are meticulously crafted to thrive in the demanding conditions of agricultural settings, guaranteeing uninterrupted performance even in the most challenging scenarios.

Vioneta Agro Automation Controller

An executor of predefined automation tasks configured by the Vioneta Agro Edge Computer. It executes specific agricultural automation sequences and tasks orchestrated by the Edge Computer, contributing to enhanced operational efficiency.

Vioneta Agro Edge Computer

Equipped with Vioneta Agro OS, the Edge Computer serves as the central hub, managing multiple Automation Controllers. It orchestrates automation processes, coordinates tasks, and communicates with interconnected devices and sensors across the agricultural infrastructure.

Vioneta Agro LoRa Gateway

Acts as a central hub, enabling LoRa-based communication between the Edge Computer and interconnected hardware components, sensors, and Automation Controllers.

Vioneta Agro Cloud

A cloud-based service allowing secure remote access to the Edge Computer from anywhere, providing monitoring, data analysis, configuration, and control functionalities.

Vioneta Agro App Store

The Vioneta Agro App Store stands as a comprehensive repository comprising dockerized applications. It serves as an invaluable resource to enhance and extend the capabilities of the Vioneta Agro ecosystem.

Vioneta Agro Mobile App

An application enabling users to access and manage the Edge Computer locally or remotely. It offers a user-friendly interface for monitoring, control, and data analysis.

Vinsight

Advanced Machine Learning, Data analytics and reporting engine for predictive insights and trend reports.

Vcam

AI-driven video analysis system to detect specific events like animal intrusions.

Vassist

AI Driven Interactive assistant for decision support, allowing voice commands or text inputs to control devices and trigger automations.

About Us

Our Company

Vioneta pioneers transformative solutions at the intersection of technology and agriculture. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to sustainable farming practices, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge smart agriculture solutions.

Our mission is to revolutionize the agricultural landscape by harnessing the power of technology to address the challenges faced by farmers worldwide. Through our expertise in IoT, data analytics, edge computing, and automation, we offer a comprehensive suite of solutions tailored to optimize farming operations, increase productivity, and promote environmental stewardship.

Beyond innovative technology, our core values center around customer success and partnership. We prioritize understanding the unique needs of each farmer or agricultural enterprise, collaborating closely to deliver customized solutions and provide expert consultancy services. Our goal is to empower our clients with the tools, insights, and support necessary to thrive in an ever-evolving agricultural ecosystem.

Our Vision

At Vioneta, we recognize the critical challenges facing modern agriculture and the immense opportunities within the rapidly growing Agritech market. Our vision is to empower farmers globally, driving sustainable growth and ensuring food security through innovative technology.

Addressing Key Challenges

  • Outdated farming methods & lack of actionable data.
  • Unreliable internet connectivity in rural areas.
  • Inefficient resource usage (water, fertilizers, chemicals).
  • Significant knowledge gap in agri-tech adoption.
  • Need for affordable, offline-capable intelligent systems.

Seizing Market Opportunities

  • Global Agritech market valued at $24.5B (2023).
  • Expected to reach $49.5B by 2031 (12.33% CAGR).
  • New Zealand Agritech target: $4.8B revenue by 2030.
  • Growing global population demands increased food supply.

Our Core Strengths & Opportunities

  • Sustainable, easy-to-use, and affordable solutions.
  • Pioneering edge computing for offline insights.
  • Integrated ecosystem and data-driven analytics.
  • Commitment to inclusivity for all farmers.
  • Opportunity to feed a growing global population.
